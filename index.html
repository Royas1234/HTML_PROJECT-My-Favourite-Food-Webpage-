    About

    Ofada rice is a Yoruba dish. It is the name for an indigenous rice from a small community called Ofada in Obafemi Owode Local Government Area of Ogun State. It is not exclusively grown in the community, but it is an indigenous rice grown in southwest Nigeria but named after the Ofada community. It is used in making a variety of dishes. Ofada rice are mostly blends, and some of the rice varieties in the blends are not indigenous to Africa; however, they usually also contain African rice. It is grown almost exclusively in Ogun State,a state in southwestern Nigeria. Ofada rice is traditionally served in an uma leaf , with a sauce of atarodo (spicy) and tatase (sweet) pepper, onion, locust beans, palm oil, and meat. It is a festive meal rather than an everyday type of food for most Nigerians but it is an everyday street food for the towns of Ikenne and Ilisan in Ogun state.It is also often served along with a vegetable stew that may contain locust beans as an ingredient.It is often served with ayamase stew or obe-ata-iru, both specially prepared for ofada rice consumption.

    Recipees

    Preparation time: 30mins

    Cook time: 20mins

    Feed: 2 peolple

    Ingredients Measurements
    Ofada Rice 2 tin cups
    Palm oil 2 Cooking spoons
    Red scotch bonnet pepper/Habanero pepper(Ata rodo) 4 medium-sized
    Red chilli Pepper(Sombo) 2 medium-sized
    Locust beans 1 table spoons, Rinsed
    Assorted meat; Roundabout(Intestine); Sharki(Tripe); Kpomo(Cow head) 100g of each
    Crayfish(Grounded) 1 fulled table sppon
    Beef meat 100g (cut in chunks)
    Onion 1 red onion
    Seasoning cubes/Salt 1/2 of small cube/ salt to taste

    Direction

    Ofada Rice

    1. Pick out the stones in the measured rice
    2. Wash the sorted rice with clean water severally , and place in a small pot
    3. Add some water to the pot and parboil for 10mins

    Ofada Stew

    1. Pour in palm oil inside a pot and heat for 10-13mins
    2. Add diced onion, pepper puree and iru and stir for 5minutes
    3. Add the Assorted meat stock including Assorted meat and beef, Crayfish.
    4. Taste to see if more salt is needed, stir and cover the pot
    5. Leave to simmer for 15mins
